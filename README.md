# Prediction Market Intelligence

## Overview
Prediction Market Intelligence is a concept research project exploring how prediction markets function as tools for information discovery rather than just speculation platforms. This project examines how collective positioning, probability signals, and market sentiment can be interpreted as forms of decentralized intelligence.

## Motivation
Prediction markets are often misunderstood as simple betting platforms. In reality, they can act as mechanisms for aggregating distributed knowledge and surfacing valuable signals about future events. This project explores that informational layer through research documentation and AI-assisted concept visualization.

## Objectives
- Explore how prediction markets aggregate information
- Document how probability signals can be interpreted
- Demonstrate AI-assisted research workflows
- Experiment with creator-led research concepts
- Promote transparency in AI-assisted development

## Token Concept
The project introduces a conceptual coordination token intended to represent early participation in prediction market research experiments. The token is designed as a signaling mechanism for research participation rather than a financial instrument.
